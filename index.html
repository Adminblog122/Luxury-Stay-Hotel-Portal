About EliteStay

EliteStay was founded on a simple principle: Luxury shouldn't be hard to find, and hospitality should be personal.

Since 2010, we have curated a collection of over 500 properties across 40 countries. Our team of travel scouts visits every location in person, ensuring that the "luxury" tag on our site truly means excellence in every thread count and sunset view.

Our Mission

To eliminate the stress of travel planning by providing a human-first, verified platform for booking the world's finest stays. We believe in transparency, speed, and the power of a perfect morning view.
